Delete Your Account

How to request deletion of your Breeze account and associated data.

Delete from the app

1Open the Breeze app and go to Settings

2Scroll down to the Data section

3Tap Delete my account

4Confirm twice when prompted. Your account will be permanently deleted.

What gets deleted

  • Your Breeze account and authentication credentials
  • All community posts, replies, and reactions
  • Your community profile and circle memberships
  • Listener profile and peer support data
  • Buddy pairs and check-in messages
  • Group breathing session participation
  • All locally stored data (mood logs, journal entries, session history)

What is NOT stored on our servers

The following data is stored only on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app or tap "Delete all my data" in Settings:

  • Mood entries and mood history
  • Gratitude journal and freewrite entries
  • Breathing and meditation session history
  • Self-care check-ins
  • Personal settings, name, and emergency contact

This action is permanent

Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. All associated data is permanently removed from our servers. If you have an active Breeze Pro subscription, please cancel it through the Google Play Store before deleting your account.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app or need assistance deleting your account, contact us at support@breeze-app.com with the email address associated with your account.
